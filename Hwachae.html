Ingredients:

  • Assorted fruits (such as strawberries, pineapple, kiwi, orange, grapes)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cups cold water or sparkling water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fruit juice (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and prepare the fruits. Cut them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the cold water (or sparkling water) with honey or sugar. Stir until the sweetener is dissolved.
  3. Add the prepared fruits to the pitcher.
  4. If desired, add a splash of fruit juice for extra flavor.
  5. Stir gently to combine all the ingredients.
  6. Place ice cubes in serving glasses.
  7. Pour the hwachae into the glasses.
  8. Garnish with mint leaves.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
