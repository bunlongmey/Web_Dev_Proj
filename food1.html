Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1/2 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup julienned cucumber
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup spinach
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced beef or tofu
  • 1 egg
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Gochujang (Korean chili paste)
  • Sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare all vegetables by washing and cutting them into thin strips.
  3. Heat a pan over medium heat and cook beef or tofu until fully cooked.
  4. In the same pan, cook the egg sunny-side-up or to your preferred doneness.
  5. In individual serving bowls, place cooked rice at the bottom.
  6. Arrange the vegetables and cooked beef/tofu on top of the rice.
  7. Place the cooked egg on top.
  8. Drizzle with sesame oil, soy sauce, and gochujang to taste.
  9. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
  10. Mix everything together thoroughly before eating.
