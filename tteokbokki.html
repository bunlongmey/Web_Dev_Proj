Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Korean rice cakes (tteok)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup fish cakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced scallions
  • 1/4 cup sliced onion
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 boiled eggs (optional)
  • Sesame seeds for garnish
  • Roasted seaweed (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a medium-sized pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add the rice cakes, gochujang, soy sauce, and sugar to the boiling water.
  3. Stir until the sauce thickens and the rice cakes are tender, about 7-10 minutes.
  4. If using, add fish cakes, scallions, onion, and cabbage to the pot. Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  5. Divide the tteokbokki into serving bowls. Top with boiled eggs if desired.
  6. Garnish with sesame seeds and roasted seaweed, if desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
